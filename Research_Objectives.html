Research Objectives

The main research objective of the project is to develop an integrated system for optimizing coffee cultivation and improving its profitability through the following sub-objectives:

Sub-objectives are as follows;

A. Utilizing soil texture analysis to predict the most suitable coffee variety based on known specifications of each type

B. Implementing GPS location-based detection to determine the most suitable coffee variant and yield potential.

C. Forecasting future coffee export demand and price trends to enhance farmers' attitudes towards coffee cultivation and maximize benefits from the yield.

D. Developing a robust image processing system to identify and diagnose diseases in coffee plants for timely intervention and improved plant health..
